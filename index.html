Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is this a legal service?

    Yes. DreamCar can be used legally worldwide.

  • Can I cancel my paid subscription anytime?

    Yes. You can cancel your subscription any time, and you can enjoy your current benefits until the end of the month.

  • Do I get a refund in case the renter does not provide me the promised car?

    It depends, in this case please contact our support. We take matters in to our own hands with the renter and after the investigation we provide you with a solution.

  • What can I do if rentee damages my car as renter?

    Please contact our support and provide us with pictures about the damage. In case your car was covered under the insurance type we detail in our Term and Conditions. In case your claim is valid we refund you for the occurred damage.
